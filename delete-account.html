Account & Data Deletion

NCW Studios respects your right to privacy and data autonomy. Learn how to clear local data or submit a formal account deletion request.

How Data Deletion Works

1

On-Device Local Data (Routine Reminder App)

Routine Reminder stores your daily schedules, custom alarms, and habit logs locally on your device. Uninstalling the app from your device automatically removes all local data instantly.

2

Support & Registered Account Deletion

If you have registered an account, subscribed to features, or communicated with our support team, you can request full erasure of your recorded account details, email address, and associated metadata.

3

Processing Timeline

Upon receiving your deletion request, NCW Studios will process your request and permanently remove your data within 7 business days. A confirmation email will be sent upon completion.

Data Removal Overview

Data Type Action Taken Retention Period
Routine Schedules & Habits Permanently deleted upon app uninstall or request Immediate
