1. Overview

Welcome to NCW Studios ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our mobile applications, including Routine Reminder and associated developer services.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, disclosed, and safeguarded when you install, use, or interact with our mobile applications. This policy satisfies all requirements set forth under the Google Play Developer Program Policies.

Key Takeaway: Routine Reminder is engineered as a privacy-first application. Your routine schedules, habit entries, and alarm preferences are stored locally on your device and are never sold or rented to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

We minimize data collection to only what is necessary to deliver functionality.

A. Data Created & Stored by You

When you use Routine Reminder, you create local data including task schedules, habit reminders, custom alarm labels, and notification times. This information remains stored on your local device storage.

B. Automatically Collected Technical Data

Depending on system configuration and app updates, non-personally identifiable diagnostic information may be collected automatically by platform services (such as Google Play Services or Android OS), including:

  • Device model, manufacturer, and operating system version
  • App performance stats (e.g. crash logs, memory exceptions)
  • Anonymized installation identifiers

C. Support Communications

If you contact us via email at developersncwstudios@gmail.com, we receive your email address, name (if provided), and any details included in your message to resolve your support inquiry.

3. Device Permissions

To provide essential features such as alarm notifications, Routine Reminder may request the following system permissions:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS / SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: Required to trigger timely audio and visual reminders for your scheduled routines.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: Enables the app to restore scheduled alarm notifications after restarting your mobile device.
  • VIBRATE: Allows optional haptic feedback when a reminder alarm rings.

You can adjust or revoke these permissions at any time through your mobile device's system settings.

4. Data Usage & Storage

We use collected data solely for the following purposes:

  • Delivering core application functionality (triggering alarms and managing daily routine schedules).
  • Troubleshooting bugs, crashes, and improving application stability.
  • Responding to user support requests submitted to developersncwstudios@gmail.com.

We do NOT sell, lease, trade, or monetize your personal data.

5. Third-Party Services

Our applications may rely on trusted platform framework services provided by Google:

  • Google Play Services: Used for app distribution, updates, and core Android system integration (Google Privacy Policy).
  • Google Play Crash Reporting / Analytics: Helps diagnose application stability across different device hardware configurations.

6. Account & Data Deletion Rights

In accordance with Google Play's Account Deletion requirements, users have complete authority to request or execute the removal of their data.

Because Routine Reminder operates with on-device local storage, uninstalling the application immediately deletes all local routine data, alarms, and settings from your device.

If you have registered an account or communicated with us via email, you may submit a formal deletion request at any time through our dedicated portal:

Go to Account & Data Deletion Page

7. Children's Privacy

Our applications are intended for a general audience and are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please notify us immediately at developersncwstudios@gmail.com so we can take immediate corrective measures.

8. Security Precautions

We implement industry-standard administrative and technical safeguards to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. However, no method of transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we recommend keeping your device operating system updated.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect app updates, technical improvements, or regulatory changes. Any modifications will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage users to review this page periodically.
